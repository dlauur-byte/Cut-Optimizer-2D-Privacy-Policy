Privacy Policy — Cut Optimizer 2D

Last updated: June 2026

Cut Optimizer 2D is a mobile app that helps users generate optimized cutting layouts for sheet materials (wood, metal, plastic, and similar flat panels). We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app processes and how.

1) Information We Collect

Cut Optimizer 2D does not collect, store, or share personal data.

2) How Data Is Processed

All optimization calculations are performed locally on your device. No optimization data is sent to our servers.

3) Permissions

The app may use local file access only when you explicitly choose export/save actions (PDF/CSV/project files), and only to store files on your device.

4) Third-Party Services

The app does not include analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs. If you choose to open external links (e.g., YouTube demo), those services apply their own privacy policies.

5) Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6) Data Security

Because personal data is not collected by the app, no personal user database is maintained by us.

7) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if app features change. Updates will appear on this page.
