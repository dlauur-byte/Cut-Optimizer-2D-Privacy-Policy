Privacy Policy

App: Cut Optimizer 2D
Last updated: June 2026

1. Summary

Cut Optimizer 2D stores your projects locally on your device. The app also uses Google services for ads and in-app purchases, which may process certain technical data as described below.

2. Data Stored by the App

The app stores project data, stock profiles, settings, and optimization history in local app storage on your device. This local data is not uploaded to our own servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (for ads in the free/trial experience)

Google Play Billing (for upgrading to PRO / ad-free)

These services may collect and process technical information such as device/app identifiers, diagnostics, and purchase-related status according to Google's own privacy terms.

4. Internet Usage

The app may use internet access for ad delivery, billing operations, and service communication with Google Play/AdMob infrastructure.

5. Permissions

The app uses network-related permissions required for advertising and billing functionality. No contacts, location, camera, microphone, or SMS permissions are requested by the app.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
